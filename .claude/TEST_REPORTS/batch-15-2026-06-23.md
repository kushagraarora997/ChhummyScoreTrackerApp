## Key Discoveries

1. **Eliminated players ARE shown in Who Closed** (but with `disabled` prop + "ðŸ’€ OUT" badge). CLAUDE.md was ambiguous â€” behavior is intentional and correct.
2. **Numpad cap is digit-level** (not confirm-level): entering "6" then "1" immediately rejects the "1" because `Number("61") > 60`. The `Math.min(..., 60)` in the confirm handler is only a safety net.
3. **`UNTOUCHABLE` and `ICE_COLD` are independent**: a 0-point winner gets BOTH. A winner with some points (e.g., 50) who never hit 70 gets only UNTOUCHABLE.
4. **`CLUTCH_MASTER` tie check**: `leaders.length === 1` is the guard â€” correctly handles 2+ players tied for top closes.
